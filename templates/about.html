About Us

    

    

        

        Welcome to our Library Database Management System website! Libraries play a large role in providing access to knowledge and encouraging learning.
        For this reason it is important to efficiently and effectively manage library data.

        Database Management Systems (DMS) are essential tools for libraries as they help to handle, update, and store library data such as user types,
        book information, security, and book movement. Our DMS has been designed to simulate a library application that will help users handle, update,
        and store library data. This includes user types, book information, security, and book movement.
