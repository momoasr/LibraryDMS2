About Us

    

    

        

        Welcome to our Library Database Management System website! Libraries play a large role in providing access to knowledge and encouraging learning.
        For this reason it is important to efficiently and effectively manage library data.

        Database Management Systems (DMS) are essential tools for libraries as they help to handle, update, and store library data such as user types,
        book information, security, and book movement. Our DMS has been designed to simulate a library application that will help users handle, update,
        and store library data. This includes user types, book information, security, and book movement.
        

    

    

    
    

            
            
User Guide

            
            

                Search
                

                    

                    In the upper right hand corner,
                    click on the dropdown menu. Select your 
                    search prefrence and enter your search in the field.
                    

                

            

            

            

            Registration
            

            
Within the navigation bar click on "Register".
                Fill out the form and create a password.
                Once completed and submitted you will be given 
                your libary card number.
            
  
            

            

            

            

                Log-In
                

                    

                    Within the navigation bar click on "Login".
                    Enter your libary card number and the password you created.
                    Click login.
                    

                

            

            

            

                Log-Out
                

                    

                    Within the navigation bar click on "Logout".
                    

                

            

            

            

                Recover Password
                

                    

                    From the login page click "Forgot Password".
                    Once on the password reset form enter in your
                    libary card number and personal information.
                    click submit and your password should reset.
                    

                

            

            

            

                Change Account information
                

                    

                    Log in to your account using your credentials. 
                    From your account click "Account details" & "Change Account Info".
                    Fill out ALL fields within the form with updated information,
                    and validate with your libary card number.
                    

                

            

            

            

                Change Password
                

                    

                    Log in to your account using your credentials. 
                    From your account click "Account details" & Forgot Password.
                    Validate your request with you libary card number, & credentials.
                    Enter a new password and confirm it.
                    

                

            

            

            

                Rent A Book
                

                    

                    Log in to your account. Search for a book either from the home page
                    or members page. Click the option to rent the selected book. 
                    The selected book will now display on your profile page as being rented.
                    

                

            

            

            

                Return Rental
                

                    

                    Log in then click the “Return rental” button on your profile page.
                    

                

            

            

            

                Request Account Deletion
                

                    

                    Log in to your account using your credentials. 
                    From your account click "Account details" & Request Account Deletion.
                    You will lose your account but be eliglble to reinstate it.
